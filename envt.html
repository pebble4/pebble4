Importance of the Environment

Nature is Earth's gift that should be cared for, especially we wouldn't be what we are without it. Unfortunately not everyone takes care of the natural beauty that surrounds them and for that reason our objective is to make people realize the importance of our habitat therefore everyone can contribute in a way.We want to share some of the most current evironmental events in Florida with you.Help us in our mission, share your ideas and make a change.

Florida conservation coalition

The Florida Conservation Coalition is devoted to protecting and conserving Florida’s land, fish and wildlife and water resources that are essential to the well-being and quality of life of the residents of this state, and for its long-term economic prosperity.

Environment Florida

Environment Florida is a citizen-based environmental advocacy organization and a project of Environment America. Funded by supporters like you, we research the challenges confronting our environment and educate the public about what’s at stake. Through our research reports, news conferences, interviews with reporters, op-ed pieces, letters to the editor and more, we raise awareness of environmental issues and promote sensible solutions.

Join them»

Solar Energy Industries Association

The Solar Energy Industries Association (SEIA®) is the driving force behind solar energy and is building a strong solar industry to power America through advocacy and education.As the national trade association in the U.S., we represent all organizations that promote, manufacture, install and support the development of solar energy.
